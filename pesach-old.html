Passover Seder (Haggadah shel Pesach)

Here are audio clips of the major portions of the Haggadah that are recited during the Passover Seder - to help you learn how to better conduct your own seder at home.

Audio clips copyright ©2006 Rabbi Mark Zimmerman. All rights reserved.

* Because of the great variety of printed haggadot, you may find slight variations from the text you use.  These clips are only of sections that are typically sung. We have not recorded haggadah passages that are read aloud or simply davened (chanted) without a set melody - such as karpas, 10 plagues, hamotzi, etc.
