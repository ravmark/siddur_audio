Come Join us in Israel this Summer!

Rabbi Mark Zimmerman will be hosting an exciting mission to Israel this summer!   (Open to the community)

Click Here for more information!

Our incredible tour of Israel is scheduled to begin on June 21, 2016 and return on July 4th.
The trip is open to anyone in our community (whether or not they are members of Congregation Beth Shalom).

This promises to be a terrific summer to visit Israel, with Israel expecting a record number of visitors this year.
Some of the highlights for our tour will include:

  • Accommodations in all deluxe hotels
  • Western Wall and tunnels
  • Old City, Jewish Quarter, and the Jerusalem Cardo
  • Yad Vashem
  • Exciting Exploration of Archaeological Dig
  • Dead Sea region, including Massada and Ein Gedi waterfall
  • Jeep tour in the north - Golan Heights
  • Tel Aviv, museums, beach, and night life
  • Visit cities of Tiberius and Tzefat
  • So much more!!!

    Please RSVP to me at rabbi@bshalom.net if you are considering joining us or if you're interested in getting some additional information.
    And please feel free to share this email with family and friends who may be interested in coming along!

    B'Shalom,
    Rabbi Mark Zimmerman
